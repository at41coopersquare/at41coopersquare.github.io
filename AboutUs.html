A new way to see The Cooper Union.


Driven by the goal of working with our building tenants to improve energy use, @41CooperSquare aims to share our building's energy performance with building tenants and visitors alike. By making this information accessible to the public, we hope to encourage green behaviors, inspire support for energy efficiency, and showcase some of the unique features of The Cooper Union.



Meet the creators.


Janet Butler and Polina Smirnova are two seniors in Mechanical Engineering at The Cooper Union. Under the advisement of Professor Melody Baglione, @41CooperSquare is their senior design project.
