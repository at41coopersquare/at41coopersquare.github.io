What is cogeneration?

Typical heat engines produce electricity and dispose of waste heat. Cogeneration is a promising green technology because it uses this waste heat to either heat or cool the building through the raidant panels.

How does it work?

In winter, "waste" heat is passed through a heat exchanger for heating. During the summer, this heat is used to drive an absorption chiller.
