Cogeneration

Typical heat engines produce electricity and dispose of waste heat. Cogeneration is a promising green technology because it uses this waste heat to either heat or cool the building through radiant panels .

In winter, "waste" heat is passed through a heat exchanger for heating. During the summer, this heat is used to drive an absorption chiller .
